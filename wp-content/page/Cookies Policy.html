What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (such as a computer, tablet or smartphone) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize your device, remember your preferences and analyze your use of the website. We use cookies to improve your user experience and provide you with personalized content and services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly, helping you to move around the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas. Without these cookies, certain services we cannot provide you.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit, loading time, error messages, etc. We use this data to improve the performance of the website and the user experience.
We collect and analyze this data through Google Analytics (GA) to understand website traffic, user behavior, and content performance. GA allows us to see information such as user sources and visit length, helping us optimize the content and structure of the website.

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember choices you make (such as language or regional preference) and provide more personalised services. They may also be used to remember custom settings you have made to the site's features and enhance your experience.

Advertising and Targeting Cookies

We use cookies from Google Ads and other advertising platforms to show you personalized advertising when you browse this or other websites. These cookies collect information about your browsing habits, preferences, and clicks in order to show you more relevant advertising content.

Analytics and Optimization Cookies

We use cookies to analyze the website's content and SEO performance through SEO plugins and Google Search Console (GSC). These tools help us monitor the website's ranking in search engines, keyword performance, and indexation, so as to optimize content to improve website visibility and user experience.

How Long Are Cookies Stored?

The cookies we use are stored on your device for different periods of time. Some cookies are deleted once you close your browser (session cookies), while others remain on your device until they expire or you actively delete them (persistent cookies). The specific storage period may vary depending on the purpose of use.
